Tokenomics of Inception

Launched via MemeJob, the SATAN token follows a fair distribution model. There is no pre-sale, no venture capital, and no pre-allocated team tokens. Power rests entirely with the community from day one.

Max Supply: 1 Billion SATAN

A fixed supply ensures scarcity. The token's journey begins with its full circulation potential available to the community through the MemeJob platform.

The MemeJob Graduation Principle

Our primary objective is to foster enough community support and engagement to 'graduate' from MemeJob. Graduation is the key that unlocks the next phase: the ability to establish a liquidity pool on SaucerSwap, giving the token true market utility and a decentralized trading foundation.

Distribution Philosophy

  • ✓ 100% Community Owned: All tokens are in circulation or available via MemeJob. The community drives the value and future.
  • ✓ No Initial Liquidity Pool: The token must earn its place on a DEX through community effort. This prevents manipulation and ensures a truly grassroots launch.
  • ✓ Future Treasury: A portion of the supply will be proposed for a community-governed treasury *after* graduation and LP establishment, subject to a community vote.
